The report below is from PACER, the US Federal Court online docket. Sealed criminal cases (indictments) have been filed at 65 times the normal rate from October, 2017 through September, 2020, and will likely continue at that same pace. Over 200,000 sealed cases have been filed over that period. In the reports, sealed cases are below the non-sealed cases.
